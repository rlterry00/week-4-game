Run the Jewels!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four jewels below. By clicking on a jewel you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each jewel is hidden to you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the values of each jewel will change.
